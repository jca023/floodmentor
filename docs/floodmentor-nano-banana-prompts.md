# FloodMentor - Nano Banana Video Prompts

**Video Length:** 60-90 seconds (6-8 scenes, ~10-12 sec each)
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (vertical for social)
**Style:** Cinematic, professional, warm lighting, realistic

---

Use these consistently across all prompts for visual coherence:

- Blue polo shirt with company logo
- Khaki pants
- Short brown/gray hair
- Professional but approachable appearance
- Carries smartphone (modern iPhone/Android)
